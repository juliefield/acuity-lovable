Power Rank Leaderboard

Alongside the A-GAME Leagues tournament, you're competing against other Team Leaders based on four fun factors - measured in aggregate, as the Power Rank. Following is a description of each element and how it's scored:

  • Agent Rating: Every week, player/agents rate leader performance on a scale of 1 to 5 stars. Requests for ratings are delivered to agents at 5pm every Wednesday and the deadline to rate for the current matchup is 5pm the following Monday. Scores will not update based on ratings received after expiration! You gain a point for every star collected - up to 60 points possible every week. Pro tip: Encourage your players to check their Acuity inbox, exercise their voice, and submit their weekly ratings.
  • Attrition: Each member of your team that attrits scores -30 points. Pro tip: Engage with your players. Let them know you see them, support them, and care. They'll appreciate it and be much more likely to stick around.
  • Engagement: Select your starting lineup yourself = +30 points. Or use auto-pick = -10. Pro tip: Select your starting lineup manually - each week! Research indicates team performance averages 18% better when you do.
  • Game Score: The total points your team scores in each week's matchup are added to your total. Pro tip: consistently and creatively motivate and cheer your team to win!

Scoring is updated in real-time as actions occur or as team scores refresh. Power Rank is calculated during the regular season only (points do not accumulate during the post-season).

Bring your A-GAME, scale that leaderboard, and revel in Power Rank glory!
