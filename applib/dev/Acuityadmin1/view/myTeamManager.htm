Instructions

Remove User: In My Team, click remove and enter an effective date that the user should be removed from your team.

Add User from Pool: Under the Pool Users, find the user you would like to add, and click the Add button. Enter the effective date that they joined your team. The Effective Date cannot be in the future.

Add User from another Team: Under Other Teams, select the team the user is coming from, or enter information to find them by first/last name. Click the Add button, and enter the effective date that they joined, or will join, your team.
