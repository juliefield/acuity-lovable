1. SIGN-UP

Congratulations! You’ve been invited to play A-GAME Xtreme. The total number of teams is limited, so sign up and reserve your spot asap.

Log into Acuity, click the Xtreme tab and then click the “Join Now” button next to the League, Conference or Division in which you’d like to compete.

2. PERSONALIZE/DRAFT/MANAGE

Name Your Team

Name your team (or do it later based on input from your players) and select colors, etc.



Draft Your Team

There are two options for how your Xtreme team may be drafted – Auto or Manual pick. If you don’t have time or simply prefer a hands-off approach to creating your fantasy team, click the Auto Pick option and the system will draft your team for you. Serious players, who want to hand-pick their players, should click the Manual option.


In either mode, the roster will include an equal number of agents from low, average and high performance tiers (referred in A-GAME Xtreme as Rank Zones).


Manual Pick


If you opted to make your Xtreme team draft picks manually and are ready to create your draft queue, click the Enter Draft Room button.




The Draft Room enables you to customize your team based on location, project and performance stats and other filters. Select the players you’re interested in based on whatever criteria you like (bottom window columns are sortable). Your roster must include an equal number of agents from low, average and high performance tiers (referred in A-GAME Xtreme as Rank Zones). You’ll include extra players in your queue as alternates in cases where you lose a queued player to another Xtreme team owner during the draft process. Use the ↑ ↓ buttons to position players in the order you’d like them to be drafted,

Once your queue is full, a message similar to the one below will be displayed.



3. CONNECT

Your Xtreme team is drafted and now it’s time to play. Things to do…

Contact your players!


Let them know you care. Let them know you are there to assist, support and motivate. Let them know that your honor and pride are on the line to win.

Communicate regularly with your team

  • Log into Acuity and check their KPI and Balanced Score performance.
  • Send messages of encouragement and inspiration via Acuity Chat and Messaging modules.
  • Touch base with your players’ Supervisors to verify your team is getting the coaching and training support they need to succeed.
  • Take an active role to support and mentor your team.

4. PLAY

Playing A-GAME Xtreme is easy. Your routine will be nearly identical to that of a Supervisor's — and it goes something like this.

Setup Your Team

Log into Acuity, click the A-GAME tab.
Select Team | Team Setup

  • Name your team (get creative! Consider polling your team for suggestions or choose via contest or vote.)
  • Select team colors
  • Automatically Select Teams: Not recommended because it's no fun. If the auto option is selected, all starting lineups for the current matchup will be selected automatically (at random) from all eligible players. If this option is not selected (by the supervisor or Xtreme owner), and the window for manual roster selection/modification has past, all or whatever remaining starting lineup slots will be filled automatically at random.

Check Your Schedule

Like most leagues, be they athletic or other type, A-GAME competitions are comprised of a regular season — a series of pre-scheduled games followed by a playoff period and a league championship.

Review your schedule and make plans for aggressive smack-talk and confrontational challenges of your opponents (Acuity Chat or Messaging are excellent channels to communicate your competitive challenges and intentions. The parking lot is good, too. Keep it fun and always in good cheer! ☺)

Log into Acuity, click the A-GAME tab and Check out your team schedule by going to Xtreme clicking Team | Schedule


Select Your Starting Lineup

Prior to each A-GAME Matchup, you'll need to select a starting lineup of six players from among your full player roster. The balanced score for each starting player will be added together to determine the team score. This score will be updated each day. Keep in mind that positions may include an X Factor (weight) of either 1, 2 or 3 (i.e., balanced score for agent assigned to Quarterback position will be multiplied by a factor of 3 for purposes of A-GAME scoring!).

The process is easy:

  • Log into Acuity
  • Click the A-GAME tab
  • Click on the Xtreme menu (you may already be there by default).
  • Click Team | Roster
  • Click the pencil icon next to the player you want to add to the starting lineup and select a position.
  • Complete process for all six players.

  • You're are ready to rumble.

Monitor Progress — Manage Performance

The game has begun and you'll want to leave as little to chance as possible.

First, keep an eye out on the score.

To do so, go to:

  • Click Team | Matchup

The Matchup view will be updated every day. Connect with your team members to encourage, assist and motivate them. Remember that even though your role on the organization may be vastly different than that of the front-line agent, your overall objectives are shared and your successes are fundamentally and inextricably linked. Your insights and experience about contact center operations, people and performance strategies, life and more could make the difference between the best or worst day ever.

Use Acuity Chat or Messaging to communicate with your team. Better yet, visit regularly with them in person. Bring them a smile, a snack, a surprise.

Use Acuity to identify KPI strength and weakness and ensure they are sufficiently supported by their supervisor, HR, Quality and Training. Be their advocate and make sure they have everything they need to succeed individually and for the team! "The secret to all victory lies in the organization of the non-obvious" — Marcus Aurelius.


Monitor Progress — League

A-GAME league information is at your fingertips. Scores, Standings and Stats (individual and team) from teams within and outside your division are updated daily and can be viewed any time within Acuity.

To do so, go to League | Scores and League | Standings

For statistics, including Most Valuable and Most Improved Player, go to Stats | Stats and to view top players by Position, go to Stats | Position Play
