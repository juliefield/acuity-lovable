1. SETUP YOUR TEAM

Click the A-GAME tab and then - Team | Team Setup

  • Name your team (get creative! Consider polling your team for suggestions or choose via contest or vote.)
  • Select team colors
  • Automatically Select Teams: Not recommended because it’s no fun. If the auto option is selected, all starting lineups for the current matchup will be selected automatically (at random) from all eligible players. If this option is not selected and the deadline for manual roster selection/modification has past, all or whatever remaining starting lineup slots will be filled automatically at random.

2. CHECK YOUR SCHEDULE

Like most leagues, be they athletic or other type, A-GAME competitions are comprised of a regular season – a series of pre-scheduled games followed by a playoff period and a league championship.

Review your schedule and make plans for aggressive smack-talk and confrontational challenges of your opponents (Acuity Chat or Messaging are excellent channels to communicate your competitive challenges and intentions. The parking lot is good, too. Keep it fun and always in good cheer! ☺)

Log into Acuity, click the A-GAME tab and check out your team schedule by clicking Team | Schedule

3. SELECT YOUR STARTING LINEUP

Prior to each A-GAME Matchup, you’ll need to select a starting lineup of six players (sometimes more depending on A-GAME theme) from among your full player roster. The balanced score for each starting player will be added together to determine the team score. This score will be updated each day. Keep in mind that positions may include an X Factor (weight) of either 1, 2 or 3 (i.e., balanced score for agent assigned to Quarterback position will be multiplied by a factor of 3 for purposes of A-GAME scoring!).

The process is easy:

  • Log into Acuity
  • Click the A-GAME tab
  • Click on the Xtreme menu (you may already be there by default).
  • Click Team | Roster
  • Click the pencil icon next to the player you want to add to the starting lineup and select a position.
  • Complete process for all six players.

  • You are ready to rumble.

4. MONITOR PROGRESS – Manage Performance

The game has begun and you’ll want to leave as little to chance as possible.

First, keep an eye out on the score.

To do so, go to:

  • Click Team | Matchup

The Matchup view will be updated every day. Connect with your team members to encourage, assist and motivate them.

Use Acuity Chat or Messaging to communicate with your team. Note: Messaging can be invoked directly from the A-GAME Roster view!!! And visit regularly with your team in person. Bring them a smile, coaching, a snack, a surprise.

Use Acuity to identify KPI strength and weakness and ensure they are sufficiently supported by you, HR, Quality and Training. Be their advocate and make sure they have everything they need to succeed individually and for the team! “The secret to all victory lies in the organization of the non-obvious” – Marcus Aurelius.

5. MONITOR PROGRESS – League, Team, Individual

A-GAME league information is at your fingertips. Scores, Standings and Stats (individual and team) from within and outside your division are updated daily and can be viewed any time within Acuity.

To do so, go to League | Scores and League | Standings

For statistics, including Most Valuable and Most Improved Player, go to Stats | Stats and to view top players by Position, go to Stats | Position Play

6. CELEBRATE YOUR VICTORIES!

Coordinate with your local or global League Commissioner regarding A-GAME incentives, recognition and awards for outstanding team and individual play.

Team Recognition: At the end of each matchup (game), the team at the top of each division should be recognized. But any win or noteworthy performance can be recognized at the team level.

Individual Recognition: At the end of each matchup (game), outstanding performance at the individual level may be recognized for:

  • Position Play – top performance in an assigned position by team, division, etc. Go to A-GAME | Position Play to identify top performing players by position.
  • MVP/MIP – top performance based on balanced score and relative improvement by team, division, etc. Go to A-GAME | Stats to identify MVP and MIP players.
  • Medal of Excellence (aka MOE) – special individual and team recognition awarded by TouchPoint One (software vendor for Acuity and A-GAME) to MVP and MIP teams and individuals across all corporate A-GAME leagues.
